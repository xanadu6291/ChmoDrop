ChmoDrop Help

1. Purpose and Usage of the App

ChmoDrop is a utility that allows you to easily lock (read only)/unlock (writeable) folders by dragging and dropping them.

  1. Select "Lock (555)" or "Unlock (755)" by manipulating the checkbox.
  2. Drop the folder into the window.
  3. The folder icon will automatically change depending on the state.

3. Finder refresh button

If the folder icons are not displayed correctly, the Finder display may be cached.
You can force a redraw by pressing the "Refresh Finder" button.

4. Differences in access rights (555 and 755)

  • 755: The owner can read, write, and execute, other users can only read and execute
  • 555: All users can only read and execute (no writing allowed)

5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Q: Will this affect files?
    A: No, it will only change the permissions of the folder itself.
  • Q: Can I chmod recursively?
    A: Not supported in the current version.
  • Q: Can I write to a locked folder?
    A: Normally you can't (blocked by 555).
